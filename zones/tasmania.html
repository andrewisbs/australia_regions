Tasmania

Only GI: Tasmania GI

Production mainly in the Tamar Valley, in Northern Tasmania. Similar to Champagne or Rheingau in climate. White Grapes slim majority. Sparkling dominates. Jansz, now owned by Yalumba, but founded in 1980 as a joint venture between Louis Roederer and Heemskerk Wines, is the eldest commercial producer of sparkling wines in Tasmania (and one of the best)
